Jump Beat

Jump Beat is a mix of sidescroller and platformer games, built in the libGDX library. The game changes from sidescroller to platformer when the player touches the golden circle. Before picking up the cricle, the player has no control over the movement of their character, but is pushed forward the whole time, which means that the player has to jump at the right moment to avoid hitting obstacles. After picking up the circle, the player gains control of the movement, which means that they can freely move back and forward through the level. The aim of the game is to reach the end of the level and score as many points as possible, which the player gains by picking up coins and finding hidden chests. The points can be spent on a golden hat for the player's character. The game is available for PC and android phones.

Minebricks

MineBricks is the classic "brick breaker" game in the style of the popular game Minecraft. The aim of the game is to break all the squares on the screen without dropping the ball on the ground. The player uses the arrow keys to move the tile left, right and right so that the ball bounces off the tile. I used HTML, CSS and JavaScript to create the game. Music and images are courtesy of Mojang.

Pot pod noge

The web app Pot pod noge is an app for finding the best parking places for hikers in Tržič. The app is still under development but already contains the main functionalities. It is being developed by Matija Keber and myself. The app recommends the nearest parking places based on the information about the parking places in the municipality of Tržič, or the user can choose the start and end location, and the app will plot the route and tell you the length of the journey. The app shows the current status of the car parks, In addition, it provides an overview of past parking occupancy rates to help users plan their trip in advance. Currently, a local server, e.g. XAMPP, is required to run the application and a local OSRM server is required to run the route drawing.
